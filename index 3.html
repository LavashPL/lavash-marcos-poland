About Us

Based in Pilsen, Czech Republic, Lavash Marcos Poland brings the rich heritage of Armenian culinary traditions to Poland. Our lavash is crafted with dedication, following time-honored Armenian recipes to ensure authenticity in every bite. Perfect for wraps, snacks, or as a complement to your favorite dishes, our lavash is a staple for those seeking quality and taste.

Where to Buy

Our lavash is available in all Eurospar supermarkets across Poland. Visit your nearest Eurospar store today and experience the authentic taste of Lavash Marcos Poland.
